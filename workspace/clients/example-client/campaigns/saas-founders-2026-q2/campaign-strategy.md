# Campaign strategy — saas-founders-2026-q2

**Client:** example-client
**Owner:** Riccardo
**Send window:** 2026-05-12 to 2026-06-12
**Volume target:** 500 leads

---

## ICP (who we email)

- **Job titles:** Founder, Co-Founder, CEO
- **Company size:** 11-50 employees
- **Industry / vertical:** B2B SaaS (no e-commerce, no marketplaces)
- **Geography:** United States, Canada
- **Disqualifiers:** funded Series B+ (different buying motion), agencies, consultancies

## The bet

If we send a 2-step sequence about manual ops costs to founders of 11-50-person B2B SaaS in NA, positive reply rate will hit 4%.

## Pain we're naming

"Half my team's week disappears into ops the product was supposed to remove. I keep hiring instead of fixing the system."

## Promise / offer

- **Offer:** A 30-min teardown of one repetitive workflow with a written automation plan delivered in 48 hours, free.
- **CTA:** Reply with the workflow you'd want torn down — we'll grab a Tuesday or Thursday slot.

## Proof

- [x] Case study: cut 14 hrs/wk of manual lead handoff for {prior client name} in 3 weeks
- [ ] Demo asset
- [ ] Other

## Success criteria

- **Primary metric:** positive_reply_rate
- **Threshold:** 4%
- **Min sample:** 500 leads
- **Bounce ceiling:** 3%

## Risks / open questions

- Founders may not be the operational decision-maker — could end up with COO referrals instead of replies. Acceptable, but track separately.
- Proof point is from a different industry (e-commerce ops, not SaaS). May need to swap before send.
